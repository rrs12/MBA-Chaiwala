We've all heard of college dropouts who went on to become extremely successful entrepreneurs — Steve Jobs, Michael Dell, and Bill Gates, to name a few. But have you ever heard of a student who abandoned his MBA program to open his own tea stall? This is the tale of Prafull Billore, who went from wishing to study at IIM-A to getting invited as a guest speaker at the same university!
