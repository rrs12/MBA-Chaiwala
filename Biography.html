WHO IS PRAFULL BILLORE???

Prafull Billore is an Indian entrepreneur who became a millionaire all over India at the age of 22 by selling tea. Mr. Billore was born in Indore on January 14, 1996, and grew up in Indore, MP. He grew up in a Brahmin family where he used to live with his parents and his brother. He went to Gujarat for his CAT preparation but eventually gave up the preparation to pursue his passion. He chose Chai as his business as he repeatedly failed his MBA preparation and Chai was the only one who kept him awake while he prepared for his CAT.

PRAFUL'S THIRST FOR MBA

Like his friends, Praful was eager to earn his MBA from top schools in India. The deciding factors for his decision were solely the lucrative salary and opportunities offered by an MBA. His parents also wanted his son to take the MBA entrance exam and be accepted into the country's premium management institutes. Despite spending three grueling years preparing for the CAT, he failed the exam thrice. He was shocked and depressed because his school dream had failed. Praful wanted a break from everything he was doing. He packed his bags and set out to travel across the country. After touring several cities, Praful finally decided to settle in Ahmedabad. He enrolled at a random MBA school, stayed for seven days, and then departed. He claims that the criteria he was looking for were not met. He then started working at McDonald's for INR 200 per hour worked, which is roughly Rs. 6000/month.
