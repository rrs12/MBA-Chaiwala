WHY HIS STORY???

At last, I would like to share the reason why I wanted to work on this project, I am preparing for CAT and the journey has made me connect with Mr. Prafull Billore because like any other aspirant student, he too wanted to pursue an MBA and study at a top B-school in India. He attempted the CAT Entrance Exam three times and failed each time. He used to study for his CAT for more than 8 hours every day in order to get into the right MBA college where he could pursue his dreams, and his parents had high hopes for him. (Same is the case with me.) Because of his repeated failures, he decided to abandon his CAT preparation and travel throughout India in search of solace. This part has made me realize that to have a good life it’s not necessary to pass from some top B-Schools; what is important is learning and gaining skills and do what you love!

The story of Prafull Billore has not yet ended and has various milestones yet to achieve but whatever he has done so far inspires many! Personally, while doing this assignment, I have learned a lot from his experiences; the ups. The downs, the unique marketing tactics that he deployed like Free Chais for singles. It won’t be wrong to say that I have started to follow my passion rather than doing things that do not make me happy. Mr. Billore provided a glimpse into the pitching world. It basically presents a well-rounded experience in which we received practical experience of selling our idea and networking with like-minded entrepreneurs, as well as theoretical knowledge of what it takes to establish a business venture. I have started to work on being persistent and optimistic just like him and this project has also helped me to learn how to make websites and portray a report in a whole new creative way. At last, the main thing that I have learned is: You work, you fall, but what is most important is, you get up!
