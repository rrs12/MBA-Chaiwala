WHAT IS MBA CHAIWALA ALL ABOUT???

MBA Chaiwala started with bootstrap on the streets of Ahmedabad on 25th July 2017. Nothing like that is selling tea is so wonderful that you cannot sell chai with drones, chai is extremely common and highly available in every region of India. Prafull Billore at MBA Chaiwala, provides many types of Chai and serves in a unique style, with an emphasis on the unique communication behind the networking aspect. So, what is occurring at MBA Chaiwala is that they are not selling chai via this, but they are selling networking, friendship, emotion, and inspiration to individuals. Prafull's contribution to MBA Chaiwala was distinct from other chaiwalas across India. The basic vision behind MBA Chaiwala is to sell chai in a unique way: Nobody used to sell tea on the roadside in Ahmedabad using kulhad, tissue paper, a tray, and toast. The uniqueness of Prafull communicating with his customers in English turned out to be a USP for him.

Mr. Billore Ahmedabad Chai Wala has organized numerous events [`FREE CHAI FOR SINGLES` on Valentine's Day; Poetry / Singing Nights `MEHFIL E KAVITA`, many sessions organized by honorable personalities who educated young people about ENTREPRENEURSHIP; Fundraising campaigns for cancer patients, disadvantaged people, Kerala flood relief funds. And after two years the first cafe was opened in Vastrapur. They offer 50+ Chai varieties ranging from Rs. 15- Rs. 50.

The philosophy behind MBA Chaiwala is to portray the vibrant and diverse atmosphere, express poetry, hum Bollywood songs, tea pong, and other unique CHAI games, newspaper stands, empty paint container seats, and make jokes. And a general atmosphere that has good feeling, joy, and warmth at the same time. It has year-round national trending campaigns and an engaging word-of-mouth and marketing campaign.

MBA Chaiwala has campaigned politically with the governments of Delhi and Bihar. The entire brand has been recognized, discussed, and recognized by major media outlets such as BBC, CNN, ScoopWhoop NDTV, TEDx, Aaj Tak, ABP, CNBC, Brut, TOI, ZeeNews, Times Now, etc. He has been invited to speak at IIM, IIT, Josh Talks, TEDxMDIGurgaon, TEDxKIET [along with NITIN GADKARI], Lovely Professional University, and other educational institutions. Along with other world-famous entrepreneurs, he was also published in the book ALL YOU NEED IS JOSH. MBA Chaiwala provides chai counters at weddings, social gatherings, and corporate events. They want to offer tea to every business person and every Indian, and they have no plans to make this a million-dollar firm; they are not interested in money. They just want to inspire individuals, especially young people, to establish their own businesses.

They also have a franchise model of business wherein they are already present in Ahmedabad, Bhopal, Chandigarh, and Kolkata.






MBA Chaiwala had sales of Rs. 3 crore in 2019-20 with a 300 sq. ft restaurant and 25-30 employees.
